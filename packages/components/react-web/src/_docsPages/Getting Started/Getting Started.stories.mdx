# Getting started with the Compassion Design System

You don't have to be a designer or a developer to be an ally of the Compassion Design System! Read on to find out how you can be a champion of neighbour-centered digital design no matter your role.

# Using the Digital Design Kit

_If you're a designer or developer, visit one of these pages for onboarding info specific to your needs:_

- [Onboarding for Designers](https://www.notion.so/Onboarding-for-Designers-f86df0a51329413fb84796528bbaafaf)
- [Onboarding for Developers](https://www.notion.so/Onboarding-for-Developers-79a92c1a45db4ac6bfe7e0386208223a)

## Browse the Digital Design Kit

Learn more about the pieces of the Digital Design Kit and how they work together! [Check out the Figma library](https://www.figma.com/file/swCfF1h7BFNPRROTjLW7i0/Compassion-Design-System-%CE%B2?node-id=103%3A7) and browse through the documentation inside.

# Contribute to the Digital Design Kit

_If you're a designer or developer, check out contribution guidelines tailored specifically for you:_

- [How to Contribute - For Designers](https://www.notion.so/How-to-Contribute-For-Designers-befaec3a0de8430b800b40f15c1525c9)
- [How to Contribute - For Developers](https://www.notion.so/How-to-Contribute-For-Developers-83c7217888ef4722a4ced04555af6d50)

## Connect and get involved!

Whether you're a marketer, a PM, a creative type, or just someone with a vision for improving a neighbour's experience, _we'd love to learn more about your idea!_ Reach out to us via email or on Teams to start the conversation. We'll walk alongside you to flesh out your contribution and explore adding it to the Design System.

- [Join us on Teams](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/team/19%3ab931ee38989d4645866ad97918692180%40thread.tacv2/conversations?groupId=6bc4beeb-823e-483d-8f65-512f2602dbbb&tenantId=d3c08eb3-7c4c-4a01-bc01-6c67b56c8da9)
- [Email](globalux@us.ci.org)
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com/@compassion)
- [GitHub](https://github.com/compassionxd)
