# Flag

Compassion partners with frontline churches in 25 nations around the world, in
addition to operating national offices in 13 countries. Use these flags to make
a visual, recognizable connection between your content and the countries it may
relate to.

**Before you begin a design,** note that there are special considerations in
certain countries where Compassion operates. These countries have strict rules
and regulations for Christian nongovernmental organizations (NGOs), including
Compassion. Please reference the
[Country-Specific Sensitive Messaging Standards](https://compassion.media/Styleguide/messaging/#page/2D760E25-6DAF-4F58-AA6B4E192B63133D)
section of the Communications Manual for country-specific detail.

Communicating how Compassion works in these countries relies on truthful, yet
careful wording and messaging. Please communicate to supporters that the local
church continues to serve and love these children regardless of their faith.

## Sizing

Flags will grow to fill their container by default. To constrain the size of a
Flag, either give a size to its parent or add a `height` or `width` prop to the
Flag.
